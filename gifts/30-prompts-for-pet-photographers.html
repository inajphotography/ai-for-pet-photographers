  1. Open Claude Code from your my-ai-assistant folder. That's where your CLAUDE.md lives. Claude reads it automatically, which is why these prompts work better than anything you've pasted into ChatGPT.

How to Make These Even Better

Stack prompts. After running prompt 01 (5 captions), say:
"Now take caption #3 and turn it into a reel script using prompt 05's structure."
Audit your output. After Claude writes anything, ask:
"Audit this against my CLAUDE.md voice. Where does it sound generic or like a stock photographer? Rewrite those lines."
Train Claude on your real voice. Paste 3 of your actual best-performing captions and ask:
"Analyse the patterns in how I write — sentence length, opener style, emotional anchors, CTA style. Use that pattern when you write for me from now on."
Save what works. When Claude nails a piece, copy it into a voice-examples.md file in the same folder. Tell Claude to reference it. Your AI assistant gets sharper every time you do.
